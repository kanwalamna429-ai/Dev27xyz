Building the Web,
One Template at a Time

Dev27 is a passion project built by a frontend developer with a love for clean design and even cleaner code. What started as a personal collection of UI experiments grew into a library of free, production-ready templates used by thousands of developers worldwide.

Every template on Dev27 is hand-crafted — no generators, no bloated frameworks, no hidden paywalls. Just solid HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JS that you can open, customize, and ship.

Zero Dependencies

No jQuery. No bloat. Every template is built with pure HTML, CSS, and Vanilla JS for maximum performance.

Always Free

Free for personal and commercial use. No license fees, no paywalls, no surprises. Download and build.

Design-First Quality

Every template is crafted with UI/UX principles in mind — not just functional, but genuinely beautiful.
